Paul L. E. Grieco

Professor of Economics

The Pennsylvania State University

I am an economist at The Pennsylvania State University specializing in industrial organization. My research interests include the estimation of demand models, production functions, and entry models. I have worked on applications relating to health economics, retail economics, international trade, and the automotive industry.
